Safety First

Three tiers of confidence

Every item Full Disk Clean finds is classified by risk level, so you always know what you're removing.

Safe

Safe to remove

Caches, logs, temp files. Regenerated automatically by your apps. Always safe to remove.

System logs, browser caches, build artifacts

Caution

Worth a quick review

Large downloads, old device backups, Docker data. Useful to clean, but take a moment to review what you’re removing.

iOS backups, old downloads, Xcode simulators

Danger

Explicit confirmation required

Application data and system files that could affect app behavior. Full Disk Clean asks you to confirm twice before touching these.

App databases, system preferences, mail data
